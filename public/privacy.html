Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

HotS Overlay ("the Extension") is a Twitch extension that displays Heroes of the Storm match data as a real-time overlay on a broadcaster's stream. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

The Extension collects and processes the following data:

  • Heroes of the Storm replay data: Match results, hero picks, game mode, map, duration, talent builds, and XP timelines. This data is extracted from .StormReplay files uploaded by the broadcaster's desktop client.
  • Player identifiers: In-game player names and toon handles (Blizzard's anonymous player identifiers, e.g. 2-Hero-1-12345) as they appear in replay files.
  • Twitch viewer identity (minimal): When viewers interact with the extension, Twitch provides an opaque user ID and channel ID via JWT. We do not store these identifiers.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect real names, email addresses, or other personal contact information.
  • We do not collect Twitch login credentials or passwords.
  • We do not track viewer behavior, browsing history, or use analytics/tracking cookies.
  • We do not collect payment or financial information.
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties.

3. How We Use Data

All collected data is used solely to:

  • Display match history, win/loss statistics, and talent builds on the broadcaster's stream overlay.
  • Deliver real-time updates to viewers when new games are played.

4. Data Storage & Retention

Match data is stored in a MongoDB database hosted on Microsoft Azure. Data is retained indefinitely to provide historical match statistics. Broadcasters can request deletion of their data by contacting us.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share data with any third parties. Match data displayed through the extension is visible to viewers of the broadcaster's channel, consistent with the Extension's purpose as a public overlay.

6. Security

All communication between the extension and our servers uses HTTPS/WSS encryption. Upload endpoints require Bearer token authentication. The server enforces rate limiting to prevent abuse.

7. Your Rights

If you are a broadcaster using the Extension, you may:

  • Request a copy of all data associated with your account.
  • Request deletion of all your match data.
  • Uninstall the Extension at any time from your Twitch dashboard.

8. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the Extension constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
